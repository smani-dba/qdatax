Case Studies

Case Study: Revamping a Retailer's Sales Reporting

The Problem:

A mid-sized retailer with 15 stores was manually pulling sales data from their SQL Server and compiling it into Excel. This process took over a day each week, and the reports were often outdated by the time they were distributed, leading to poor inventory decisions and missed sales opportunities.

The Solution:

Using the Data Foundation Kickstart, we connected their SQL Server to Microsoft Fabric and built a core set of real-time sales and inventory dashboards in Power BI. The entire process was automated and completed in under two weeks.

The Outcome:

The finance and operations teams now have instant access to accurate data, enabling them to make smarter decisions about stock levels. The COO reported a 15% reduction in overstocked items within the first quarter, directly impacting profitability.

Case Study: Unifying Data for a Manufacturing Company

The Problem:

A small manufacturing company with 50 employees struggled with scattered data. Their SQL Server tracked production, but sales data lived in a separate system, and customer feedback was in a spreadsheet. This made it impossible to get a complete picture of their business.

The Solution:

We engaged with the Data Consolidation Blueprint, unifying all their data sources into a single Fabric Lakehouse. We then built a dimensional data model that served as a "single source of truth" for all departments.

The Outcome:

The company can now analyze the entire customer journey, from initial order to delivery and feedback. This newfound clarity led to a new product line after identifying a key customer request, resulting in a 10% increase in revenue.

Case Study: Improving Operational Efficiency for a Wholesale Distributor

The Problem:

A wholesale distributor relied on complex, slow-running queries to generate daily operational reports from their SQL Server. The queries often slowed down their core business applications, especially during peak hours.

The Solution:

We implemented the Data Foundation Kickstart to offload all reporting to Microsoft Fabric.

The Outcome:

The daily operational reporting is now automated and runs without impacting the performance of their core systems. The IT manager reported a significant improvement in database performance and the operations team now receives their reports hours earlier, improving efficiency and reducing manual effort.
